Check your organisation is eligible?

To be eligible to apply to the Community Ownership Fund you must be:

  • legally incorporated (for this round of funding we will not fund unincorporated organisations)
  • set up to deliver charitable purpose, social purpose or public benefit
  • an asset-locked organisation
  • able to show you are accountable to the local place and/or community you represent
What is an asset-locked organisation?

An ‘asset-lock’ requires that the organisation’s assets (including profit generated) are restricted for charitable purpose or community benefit.

This includes restrictions on any future sale or transfer of assets, to ensure they are used for community benefit or charitable purpose.
