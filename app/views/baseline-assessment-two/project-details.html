Help me with guidance on scoring this section

Applications will be assessed on the following 4 criteria:

1. Strategic case: applicants should show that without intervention the asset would be lost to the community, the impacts that this would have and support they have from the community and other partners.

2. Management case: applicants should show the objectives and deliverability of the project and how the asset or amenity will be run sustainably.

3. Potential to deliver community benefit: using the outcomes framework, applicants should show how the asset will deliver community benefits under community ownership.

4. Added value of the community asset based on community need: using local data and information on community need, applicants should be able to show the added value of the asset or amenity to the community.

Project description

A lack of integrated investment together with out of town retail and residential growth has led to different areas of Hedgehill city centre being less well connected, and major assets like the historic area less well used than it should be. Overall the Hedgehill city centre offer has not kept pace with increased demand for city centre living and new cultural and leisure experiences to replace rapidly declining retail.

As with many cities, the role that Hedgehill has played in the lives of its residents and the much larger population it serves has changed over the years and is continuing to do so.

A lack of integrated investment together with out of town retail and residential growth has led to different areas of the town being less well connected, and major assets like the historic area less well used than it should be. Overall the city centre offer has not kept pace with increased demand for city centre living and new cultural and leisure experiences to replace rapidly declining retail.

Over the last year residents, businesses and a range of local institutions have worked together, through Hedgehill Connections and Hedgehill city, to set clear priorities and a vision for Hedgehill that delivers:

  • New opportunities for skills and jobs for our young people and all those affected by Covid-19  
  • Growing innovative businesses  
  • A repurposed town centre with new experiences and enterprise  
  • A high-quality residential and leisure offer in the historic town core and riverfront  
  • A sustainably connected town
