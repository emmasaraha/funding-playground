Help me with guidance on scoring this section

Applications will be assessed on the following 4 criteria:

1. Strategic case: applicants should show that without intervention the asset would be lost to the community, the impacts that this would have and support they have from the community and other partners.

2. Management case: applicants should show the objectives and deliverability of the project and how the asset or amenity will be run sustainably.

3. Potential to deliver community benefit: using the outcomes framework, applicants should show how the asset will deliver community benefits under community ownership.

4. Added value of the community asset based on community need: using local data and information on community need, applicants should be able to show the added value of the asset or amenity to the community.

This project will meet the following policy aims:

  • Level up skills
  • Increase trade and prosperity

Level up skills

The company will create an educational space in the new development to continue an initiative engaging children in the local community. Our Wonkapreneur's scheme is a voluntary program inviting young people from the constituency to visit the factory to learn about the manufacturing process, and be educated in business skills.

Trade and prosperity

Our project will deliver new jobs in manufacturing, and our workforce uniform will stimulate production of white dungarees in the local area. Increased production of our fizzy lifting drinks will provide an invaluable public service by simplifying the cleaning of high-rise windows.We forecast that by 2030:

  • 5,000 new staff will be employed from the local area
  • Visitor numbers to the factory tour will increase by 200%
  • Net zero targets will be reached for this and three neighbouring companies
