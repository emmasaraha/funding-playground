Labels and probability of change

Our confidence relates to the level of risk. These tags are high (colour coded red), medium (yellow) and low (green). They’re based on 3 things:

  1. The range of participants we’ve researched with.
  2. The design pattern’s prevalence – who else uses it?
  3. Our understanding of the question, why we're asking it and how we use it.

These tags quickly illustrate how confident we are with an interaction – what parts of the service should not change, could change or definitely will change.

  • No research, changes are probable = High
  • Some research done, some changes are possible = Medium
  • Thoroughly researched, confident in pattern = Low
